Free-form placement is the default. Structure is opt-in:
- Push two views together — they snap into a tiled group.
- Pull one away — it is free again.
- Groups can be named, collapsed, folded into an icon for later restoration.
- The workspace remembers arrangement as part of its serialisable state.

This is neither tiling-first nor floating-first. Spatial placement is the primitive. Tiling is a transient grouping gesture.
