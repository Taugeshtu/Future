# Purse

> The intermediate space between finding a thing and acting on it.

Context is the enumeration of things you are attending to. Purse makes that enumeration explicit and visual.

Between a filename-and-icon and opening a file in an application, there is a missing layer: **examination**. Purse is that layer. Hold items to inspect before committing to a verb — batch-preview text files to find the right one, stage a selection before deciding what to do with it.

Purse is portable: it travels between [[strip]]s, not bound to any single workspace or application. When you invoke the [[launcher]], Purse contents are the inherited context.
