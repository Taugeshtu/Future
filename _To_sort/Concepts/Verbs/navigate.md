#verb

Miller columns: each level of the hierarchy is a flat vertical list. Left-to-right is general to specific. Up/down adjusts trajectory at the current level. The leftmost column is the broadest context; the rightmost shows files.

**Folders and files are separated.** Folders occupy the navigational columns; files appear only in the rightmost column. Folders are the trajectory; files are the destination. Mixing them creates friction.

Bookmarks are the most common entry points. You rarely navigate from root.
